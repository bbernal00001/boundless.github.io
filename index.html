Sweet.

Definitions
Adjective:
~Having the taste or flavor characteristic of sugar, honey, etc.
~Pleasing to the mind or feelings.
(That's a sweet haircut!)
Adverb:
~In a sweet manner; sweetly.
Noun:
~Something that is sweet or causes or gives a sweet flavor, smell, or sound.
~A food (such as a candy or dessert) having a high sugar content.
